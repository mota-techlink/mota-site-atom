### Market Context: A ¥300B Industry with a Data Problem

China's pet market is on track to surpass **¥312.6 billion by 2025** and **¥405 billion by 2028**. The fastest-growing segment is smart hardware—intelligent pet products accounted for **20% of the total pet supplies market in 2024**, with companion robots and health devices leading in growth rate. The demographic driving this is young: post-90s and post-00s owners represent the majority of new pet adopters.

Yet despite explosive growth, the industry suffers from structural inefficiencies:

*   **Fragmented Data:** Each hardware brand operates in a silo. A GPS collar from brand A cannot share data with a health monitor from brand B, leaving owners with disconnected dashboards and no unified health picture.
*   **High Cost of Professional Care:** Veterinary consultations are expensive and often inaccessible; owners resort to unverified online forums for health questions.
*   **Inaccurate Generic AI:** General-purpose LLMs lack the domain specificity to answer niche pet care questions accurately—breed-specific nutrition, species-level disease patterns, or behavioral anomalies require purpose-built training data.
*   **Passive Monitoring:** Current solutions are reactive. They record; they do not advise.

### The Solution: Hardware as Sensing Entry Points + 7 Proprietary AI Models

Agility's architecture is built on a deliberate principle: **hardware exists to feed data into AI, not to be the end product**. Three smart devices act as always-on sensing nodes that continuously supply multimodal data to a unified AI layer.

#### Three Core IoT Devices

**① Smart Positioning Collar**

The primary safety layer for every pet owner's core anxiety: *will my pet come home?*

*   Real-time GPS positioning with a location accuracy of **≤ 5 meters**
*   Configurable multi-zone electronic geofences with instant breach alerts
*   Tamper-detect alarm if the collar is forcibly removed
*   One-tap remote ring to acoustically locate a hiding pet

**② Smart Pet Robot Car**

An interactive mobile companion that eliminates the spatial limit of pet ownership. While the owner is at work, the car can be driven remotely via smartphone to move through the home, maintaining visual presence and two-way interaction with the pet. Beyond companionship, the onboard HD camera captures high-quality video and photo content that feeds directly into the GenAI content pipeline.

**③ Pet AI Monitor**

A stationary always-on health and behavioral surveillance device:

*   Continuous behavior and vocalization capture across 20+ classified behavioral patterns
*   Image-based analysis of **stool and vomit characteristics** (color, shape, consistency) for early-warning detection of digestive disorders or toxin ingestion
*   Litter box anomaly detection—abnormal frequency, output, or duration triggers a health alert

### Seven Vertical AI Models

The proprietary model layer is what differentiates Agility from platforms that simply aggregate sensor data.

#### Model 01 — Pet Feature Recognition
*   Identifies **100+ cat and dog breeds** with **≥ 95% accuracy** and 80+ exotic pet species
*   Recognizes **8 emotional states** (happy, anxious, fearful, playful, etc.) and **20+ behaviors** (destructive, grooming, hunting, resting)
*   Multi-dimensional personality and health assessment reports from a single photo

#### Model 02 — Physical Health Vision Analysis
Computer vision applied to four anatomical areas—**eyes, skin, oral cavity, and ear canal**—with AI-generated health status reports. The system also analyzes excretory outputs for early indicators of systemic disease, enabling owners to flag concerns before clinical symptoms become apparent.

#### Model 03 — Pet Knowledge Q&A (Veterinary LLM)
*   Backed by a **million-entry domain knowledge base**
*   Available **24/7** for intelligent symptom queries and general pet care Q&A
*   Simulates veterinary diagnostic logic to generate preliminary assessments and care recommendations—not a replacement for clinical care, but a significant first-line triage tool

#### Model 04 — Smart Photo Album Classification
Automatically recognizes pets in photos by behavior (feeding, playing, sleeping) or breed, then organizes a multi-pet household's photo library without manual tagging. Relevant for the growing segment of owners with 2+ pets who generate hundreds of photos weekly.

#### Model 05 — Generative AI Entertainment
Applies **image-to-image** and **image-to-video** generation to create:
*   Custom pet outfit visualizations
*   Meme and sticker packs
*   Scripted short-form video content ("pet sitcoms") for social media publishing
This creates a direct link between hardware-captured content and monetizable social media output.

#### Model 06 — Pet Communication & Translation
*   Recognizes and classifies pet vocalizations (meows, barks, whines by context)
*   Converts vocalizations to natural language text
*   AI generates empathic first-person responses *in the pet's voice*, simulating two-way dialogue between owner and pet

#### Model 07 — Anti-Loss System
Integrates all positioning, behavioral, and network data into a comprehensive safety layer:
*   GPS with ≤ 5m precision + 72-hour historical route replay
*   Multi-region electronic geofence configuration
*   **Mutual aid lost-pet network**: broadcasts to nearby registered users when a pet is reported missing

### Closed-Loop Scenario Examples

**Scenario A — Park Walk → Escape Alert → Recovery**

1. Collar detects geofence breach → push alert to owner's phone
2. Owner activates two-way audio; speaks to calm the pet
3. Pet responds → audio streamed back to owner
4. GPS route + audible ring function guides owner to precise location

**Scenario B — Home Alone → Health Anomaly → Owner Notification**

1. AI Monitor detects litter box visit outside normal frequency range
2. Model 02 analyzes captured image; flags abnormal stool color
3. System pushes health alert with preliminary diagnostic context
4. Owner consults Model 03 (Vet Q&A) directly from the alert notification

**Scenario C — Robot Car Capture → GenAI → Social Post**

1. Robot car is driven while owner is remote; captures a playful moment
2. Smart Album auto-tags the clip (behavior: "play", breed confirmed)
3. Model 05 generates a meme overlay + background music suggestion
4. Owner one-tap publishes to Douyin/Xiaohongshu

### Why This Is a Model for AI + Chinese Manufacturing

Agility's architecture exemplifies the MOTA TECHLINK thesis: **vertical AI combined with China's hardware supply chain advantage creates defensible, high-value products**.
