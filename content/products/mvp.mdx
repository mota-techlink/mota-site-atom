### About This Service

The AI era has democratized software creation — but there is still a real gap between ideas in the digital world and physical products you can hold in your hand.

**MOTA MVP Hardware Prototyping** bridges that gap. By combining global supply chain resources, China's unmatched manufacturing efficiency, and streamlined international logistics, we make it possible for makers, independent developers, and early-stage startups to go from **idea to physical prototype** faster and cheaper than ever before.

Whether you're an engineer with a complete design file set who needs rapid physical output, or a founder with only a product concept who needs end-to-end engineering support, we have a tier that fits exactly where you are.

### Key Advantages

* **China Manufacturing × Global Delivery**: Our Shenzhen partner network covers PCB fabrication, SMT assembly, 3D printing, and component sourcing — delivering prototypes at globally competitive costs via DHL/FedEx directly to your door.
* **Full-Stack Engineering Capability**: From circuit design and PCB layout to industrial modeling and embedded firmware, our in-house engineering team covers every layer of hardware prototyping. Standard and Premium tiers include direct engineering support.
* **Tiered to Your Stage**: Three tiers match three stages of readiness — complete files at $99, engineering review needed at $999, and starting from scratch at a custom quote. No bloated packages, no wasted spend.
* **Transparent Progress Tracking**: Once your project starts, we provide real-time status updates and flag engineering issues as they arise, regardless of your timezone.
* **Production-Ready Handoff**: Standard and Premium deliveries include a complete engineering package — design files, firmware, BOM, and production documentation — lowering the barrier to mass production or pilot runs.

### How It Works

1. **Align on Requirements**: Submit your product description and design files via the contact form. We respond within 1 business day with a recommended tier, scope confirmation, and delivery timeline.
2. **Engineering Preparation**: Basic moves directly to production. Standard begins with DFM review and BOM optimization. Premium kicks off a full design and development process.
3. **Manufacturing & Assembly**: PCB fabrication, SMT assembly, 3D printing, firmware flashing, and full device assembly are completed at our Shenzhen partner facilities under quality control.
4. **Testing & Acceptance**: Basic receives power-on functional verification. Standard and Premium undergo systematic functional testing with a formal test report.
5. **Pack & Ship**: Prototypes are ESD- and shock-protected, then shipped via DHL/FedEx to your address with real-time parcel tracking.
