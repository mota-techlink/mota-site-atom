## Payment Methods

### Credit Card

We accept Visa and Mastercard through **Stripe**, a PCI-DSS compliant payment processor.

- Payments are processed securely via Stripe Checkout.
- You receive a Stripe payment receipt immediately after payment.
- Supported currencies: USD (primary), with automatic conversion for other currencies.

### Cryptocurrency

We accept the following cryptocurrencies for service payments:

| Coin | Network |
|------|---------|
| USDC | Base, Ethereum |
| ETH | Ethereum |
| BTC | Bitcoin |

Instructions for crypto payments are provided at checkout. All crypto transactions are final and non-reversible.

### X402 On-Chain Payments (Instant Consultation)

For our Quick Consultation feature, we use the **X402 protocol** — an HTTP-native micro-payment standard built on the Base network.

- Connect your MetaMask wallet.
- Pay $0.10 USDC per consultation query.
- No gas fees on Base network layer (minimal transaction cost).
- Payment is processed automatically and the AI response is unlocked instantly.

## Delivery Process

1. **Order Confirmation** — You receive a confirmation email immediately after payment.
2. **Kickoff Call** — Our project manager contacts you within 1 business day to align on requirements.
3. **Development & Updates** — We send progress updates by email at key milestones.
4. **Review & Revisions** — Before final delivery, you receive a preview link (for websites) or review session (for hardware/AI).
5. **Final Delivery** — Credentials, files, or physical shipment delivered to you.

## Post-Delivery Support

All packages include a minimum warranty period:

- **Starter packages**: 1 month of free bug-fix support.
- **Standard packages**: 3 months of free bug-fix support.
- **Premium packages**: 6 months of dedicated support.

Support is provided via email. Response time: within 1 business day.

## Refund Policy

- **Before kickoff**: Full refund available within 24 hours of order placement.
- **After kickoff**: Partial refund based on work completed; please contact us.
- **Crypto payments**: Non-refundable once transaction is confirmed on-chain (please contact us before ordering if you have concerns).
