## Overview

Our AI Chatbot Service deploys a Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) chatbot on your website or platform. The chatbot learns from your product documentation, FAQs, and business content — then answers visitor questions instantly, 24/7, in multiple languages.

Unlike generic AI assistants, our chatbot is grounded in *your* knowledge base, so responses are accurate, on-brand, and up-to-date.

- **RAG Architecture** — Retrieves relevant context from your docs before generating responses, dramatically reducing hallucinations.
- **Multilingual** — Automatically responds in the visitor's language (EN / ZH / JA / KO and more).
- **24/7 Availability** — No downtime, no holidays, instant responses.
- **X402 Integration** — Support for on-chain micro-payments (e.g., $0.10 USDC per consultation) for premium AI interactions.
- **Easy Integration** — Embed as a widget or connect via REST API.
