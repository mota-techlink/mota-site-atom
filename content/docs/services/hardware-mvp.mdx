## Overview

The Hardware MVP Service transforms your product idea into a working proof-of-concept using components from Shenzhen's deep supply chain network. Whether you need a custom PCB, ESP32-based device, 3D-printed enclosure, or a fully assembled prototype, we handle the sourcing, assembly, and quality testing.

## Our Process

1. **Discovery Call** — We review your concept, specifications, and constraints.
2. **BOM & Sourcing** — We identify components and provide a Bill of Materials estimate.
3. **Design & Fabrication** — PCB layout, 3D modeling, and firmware development.
4. **Assembly & Testing** — Units are assembled and tested at our Shenzhen partner facility.
5. **Delivery** — Prototype shipped to your address with full documentation.
