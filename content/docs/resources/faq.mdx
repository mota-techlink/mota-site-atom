## Ordering

**How do I place an order?**
You can submit a free inquiry via the Contact Sales button on any product page, or order directly online. See the [How to Place an Order](/docs/getting-started/how-to-order) guide for full details.

**Can I request a custom package not listed on the products page?**
Yes. Contact us with your specific requirements and we'll prepare a custom proposal.

**Is there a minimum order value?**
Our entry-level packages start at $99. There is no minimum below that for standard packages.

## Delivery

**How long does delivery take?**
Delivery times vary by service. Website Building Starter takes 5–7 business days, Hardware MVP Basic takes 15–20 business days. See the [Payment & Delivery](/docs/services/payment-delivery) page for full timelines.

**What happens after I pay?**
You receive an order confirmation email, then a project manager contacts you within 1 business day to schedule a kickoff call.

**What if I need changes after delivery?**
All packages include a free support window (1–6 months depending on tier). During this period, bug fixes and minor adjustments are included at no charge.

## Payment

**What payment methods are accepted?**
We accept Visa/Mastercard (via Stripe), USDC, ETH, and BTC. See [Payment & Delivery](/docs/services/payment-delivery) for details.

**Is my credit card payment secure?**
Yes. All card payments are processed by Stripe, which is PCI-DSS Level 1 certified.

**Can I pay in my local currency?**
Our listed prices are in USD. Stripe handles currency conversion automatically for card payments.

**What is X402?**
X402 is an HTTP-native micro-payment protocol on the Base blockchain. We use it for $0.10 USDC per-query AI instant consultations. You need a MetaMask wallet to use it.

## Technical

**Do the websites you build work on mobile?**
Yes. All our websites are fully responsive and tested on iOS and Android.

**Can I update the website content myself after delivery?**
Yes. We hand over the Git repository and provide guidance on how to edit content files and redeploy.

**What languages are supported for multilingual websites?**
We support English (EN), Chinese (ZH), Japanese (JA), and Korean (KO) by default. Additional languages can be arranged.

## Support

**How do I contact support?**
Email us from the [Contact](/contact) page. Response time is within 1 business day.

**Do you offer ongoing maintenance contracts?**
Yes. After the free support period, we offer monthly maintenance plans. Contact us for pricing.
