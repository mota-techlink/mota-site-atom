## Overview

The [Showcase](/showcase) is MOTA TECHLINK's portfolio of completed and ongoing client projects. Browse real examples across our core service categories to see quality, scope, and results before you order.

## How to Browse Showcase

Visit [/showcase](/showcase) from the main navigation.

### Filter by Category

Use the category tabs to filter projects by type:

- **Hardware** — Physical product prototypes and IoT devices
- **Software** — Web applications and SaaS platforms
- **AI** — Chatbots, computer vision, and intelligent systems

### Search by Keyword

Use the search bar to find projects related to a specific industry, technology, or use case (e.g., "retail", "ESP32", "multilingual").

### Read the Full Case Study

Click on any showcase card to open the full case study, which includes:

- **Background** — Client's problem and goals
- **Solution** — What MOTA TECHLINK built and why
- **Results** — Key metrics and outcomes
- **Technologies** — Stack and components used
