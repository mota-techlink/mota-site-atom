## Overview

Ordering a service from MOTA TECHLINK is straightforward. You can submit a free consultation inquiry or pay online directly for standard packages.

## Option 1: Submit an Inquiry

**Best for**: Website builds, custom hardware, enterprise AI solutions, or any project where you'd like a consultation first.

1. Browse the [Products](/products) page and choose a service that fits your needs.
2. Click **Contact Sales** on the service detail page.
3. Fill in your name, email address, and a brief description of your project.
4. Submit the form — our team responds within 1 business day.

## Option 2: Order Online

**Best for**: Standard packages where you're ready to proceed immediately.

1. Go to the service detail page (e.g., [Website Building](/products/sitebuild)).
2. Select your preferred pricing tier.
3. Click **Order Now** and complete payment via:
   - **Credit Card** — Visa / Mastercard via Stripe
   - **Cryptocurrency** — USDC, ETH, or BTC

## Option 3: Quick AI Consultation (X402)

For quick questions before committing, use our instant AI consultation:

1. Click **Quick Consultation** on any product page.
2. Connect your MetaMask wallet.
3. Pay $0.10 USDC on the Base network to unlock an immediate AI expert response.

## After Placing Your Order

- You receive an **order confirmation email** within minutes.
- A project manager contacts you within **1 business day**.
- A **kickoff meeting** is scheduled to align on timeline and deliverables.
- You receive **status updates via email** throughout delivery.
