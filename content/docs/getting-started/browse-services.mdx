## How to Navigate This Website

MOTA TECHLINK's website is designed to help you explore services, review case studies, and contact our team with ease. This page walks you through the key sections.

## The Main Navigation

At the top of every page you will find the main navigation bar with the following sections:

- **Products** — All services MOTA offers: Website Building, Hardware MVP, AI Chatbot, and Scale-up Production.
- **Resources** — Case studies (Showcase), blog articles, help center, and documentation.
- **Pricing** — Quick overview of service tiers and starting prices.
- **Company** — About us, contact information, and legal pages.

## Exploring Services

### Products Page

Visit [/products](/products) to browse all available services. Each service card shows:
- A short description of what we deliver
- Starting price
- Key technologies used
- A link to the full detail page

### Service Detail Page

Clicking on any service takes you to a dedicated page with:

1. **Gallery** — Screenshots or photos of past deliveries.
2. **Pricing Tiers** — Basic, Standard, and Premium packages with feature lists.
3. **About This Service** — In-depth explanation of what you get and how we deliver it.
4. **Technologies We Use** — The tech stack behind each service.
5. **FAQ** — Answers to common questions specific to that service.
6. **Success Stories** — Related showcase projects delivered using this service.

## Showcase (Case Studies)

Visit [/showcase](/showcase) to browse completed projects. You can:

- Filter by category (AI, hardware, web, etc.)
- Search by keyword
- Click any project card to read the full case study

## Blog

Visit [/blog](/blog) to read our latest articles on AI, hardware prototyping, and business growth. Articles are available in four languages (EN / ZH / JA / KO).

## Help Center

The [Help Center](/help) features an AI assistant powered by MOTA’s RAG technology. Ask any question about our services and get an instant answer.

## Language Switching

All pages support four languages: English, 中文, 日本語, and 한국어. Use the language switcher in the top navigation to change languages at any time.
